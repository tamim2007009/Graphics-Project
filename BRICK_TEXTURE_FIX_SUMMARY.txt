BRICK TEXTURE DISTORTION FIX - SUMMARY
================================================================================

ISSUE:
------
The brick.jpg texture applied to factory walls and mall exterior walls was 
appearing distorted and pixelated instead of looking like a realistic brick 
surface. The texture appeared stretched, blurry, or excessively repeated.

ROOT CAUSE:
-----------
Excessive texture tiling values were being applied to the walls:
  - North wall:        25.0f (repeating 25 times across 50 units)
  - South walls:       12.0f (repeating 12 times across 21 units)  
  - Entrance strip:    4.0f (repeating 4 times across 8 units)
  - West/East walls:   25.0f (repeating 25 times across 50 units)
  - Roof parapets:     25.0f (repeating 25 times across 50 units)

When textures repeat too many times across a surface, individual pixels of the 
texture image become enlarged and visible, causing distortion rather than a 
realistic detailed surface.

SOLUTION APPLIED:
-----------------
Reduced texture tiling values to realistic amounts appropriate for architectural 
surfaces. The new values follow best practices for photorealistic texturing:

1. MALL EXTERIOR WALLS (scene_renderer.h):
   - North wall:        25.0f ? 2.0f  
   - South walls:       12.0f ? 1.5f  
   - Entrance strip:    4.0f  ? 1.0f  
   - West/East walls:   25.0f ? 2.0f  

2. FACTORY INDUSTRIAL WALLS:
   - Manufacturing zone: 8.0f  ? 1.5f  
   - Prayer room wall:   5.0f  ? 1.5f  
   - Gems shop wall:     5.0f  ? 1.5f  

3. ROOF PARAPETS (all sides):
   - All edges:         25.0f ? 2.0f  

RECOMMENDED TILING VALUES:
--------------------------
- Single large surface (> 10m wide):  1.0f - 2.0f  (texture once or twice)
- Medium wall (5-10m):                1.5f - 3.0f  (subtle repetition)
- Small detail/accent:                2.0f - 4.0f  (fine detail visible)
- Outdoor ground/terrain:             4.0f - 12.0f (can tile more)

WHY THIS WORKS:
---------------
Lower tiling values mean:
? Larger texture scale on the surface
? Fewer visible repetitions of the pattern
? Better definition of individual brick details
? More realistic appearance
? Textures blend better with lighting

TEXTURE FILTERING SETTINGS (Already Optimized):
-----------------------------------------------
In loadTexture() function (src/main.cpp):
- MIN_FILTER: GL_LINEAR_MIPMAP_LINEAR  (smooth at any distance)
- MAG_FILTER: GL_LINEAR                (smooth magnification)
- Wrapping:   GL_REPEAT                (seamless tiling)
- Mipmaps:    Generated               (multiple resolution levels)

These settings ensure the texture appears high-quality regardless of viewing 
distance or angle.
