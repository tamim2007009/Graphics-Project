BRICK TEXTURE ISSUE - RESOLUTION REPORT
================================================================================

PROBLEM STATEMENT:
==================
The brick.jpg texture applied to factory walls was displaying distorted and 
pixelated instead of appearing like realistic brick surfaces. Users reported 
the texture looked "distorted or different" from the original image.

ROOT CAUSE ANALYSIS:
====================
Excessive texture tiling parameters caused the texture to repeat far too many 
times across each wall surface, making individual texture pixels visible as 
blocky artifacts.

The mathematics:
- A texture with many repetitions = each repetition becomes smaller
- At 25 repetitions across 50 units ? each tile occupies ~2 units
- At screen resolution, this made bricks appear pixelated/blurry

SOLUTION IMPLEMENTED:
====================
